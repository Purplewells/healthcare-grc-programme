# Stakeholder Register
  
**Document Type:** Stakeholder Register  
**Classification:** Portfolio Case Study – Fictional Organisation  
**Version:** 1.0  

## 1. Introduction

Effective cyber security governance requires collaboration across clinical, operational, technical, and corporate functions.

Project SentinelCare involves stakeholders responsible for:

- Strategic decision-making.
- Clinical safety.
- Information governance.
- Cyber security management.
- Regulatory compliance.
- Operational resilience.
- Technology delivery.
- Supplier assurance.

Understanding stakeholder responsibilities ensures that cyber risks are owned, managed, and communicated appropriately.

## 11. Stakeholder Engagement Principles

Project SentinelCare will engage stakeholders through:

- Executive cyber risk reporting.
- Security governance meetings.
- Clinical safety reviews.
- Risk workshops.
- Policy consultation.
- Supplier assurance reviews.
- Security awareness campaigns.
- Audit and compliance reviews.

Effective stakeholder engagement ensures cyber security becomes a shared organisational responsibility rather than solely a technology function.
