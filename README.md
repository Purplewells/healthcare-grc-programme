## Executive Summary
> Project SentinelCare is a simulated cybersecurity consultancy engagement undertaken for Westbridge University Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust, a fictional NHS healthcare provider undergoing digital transformation.

The Trust has expanded its digital estate through cloud adoption, electronic patient records, connected medical devices, and third-party digital health services. While these initiatives have improved patient care and operational efficiency, they have also increased cyber risk, regulatory obligations, and governance complexity.

Following an independent cyber maturity review, several weaknesses were identified in governance, risk management, compliance, asset management, and incident preparedness. The Trust Board commissioned a Cyber Security Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) Improvement Programme to strengthen organisational resilience and provide assurance that critical healthcare services and patient information are appropriately protected.

This repository contains the deliverables produced during that engagement, demonstrating a structured approach to assessing cyber risk, implementing governance, improving compliance, and supporting executive decision-making.
